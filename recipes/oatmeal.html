Oatmeal: A wonderful start to the day

Oatmeal is such a common staple for breakkfast. It isn't everyone's favorite but it sure is healthy. It's rich in fiber and whole grains, which will give you a nice energy boost to start the day

In this recipe, I'll list out the steps to create your very own bowl of oatmeal. It's delicous and healthy, have I mentioned that yet?

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup oatmeal
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1 1/2 cup skim milk
  • Banana (optional)
