Oatmeal: A wonderful start to the day

Oatmeal is such a common staple for breakkfast. It isn't everyone's favorite but it sure is healthy. It's rich in fiber and whole grains, which will give you a nice energy boost to start the day

In this recipe, I'll list out the steps to create your very own bowl of oatmeal. It's delicous and healthy, have I mentioned that yet?

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup oatmeal
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1 1/2 cup skim milk
  • Banana (optional)
  1. Add oatmeal to small pot
  2. Add water then heat up stove to medium heat
  3. Stir occasionally until oatmeal thickens
  4. Once oatmeal is thick, turn off the stove
  5. Add oatmeal to bowl and add milk slowly while stirring with spoon
  6. Add rinsed blueberries and sliced banana to bowl, mix, and enjoy your hearty breakkfast!
