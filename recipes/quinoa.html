Quinoa

Quinoa: an ancient grain full with nutrition

Quinoa is a complete protein and rich in fiber. Give this recipe a go if you dare

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup quinoa
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 roma tomato
  • 1/8 red onion
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1/2 tablespoon olive oil
  • A pinch of salt
  1. Heat pot to medium heat and add olive oil, chopped garlic, onion, and tomato
  2. Make sure to stir occasionally to avoid sticking
  3. Once tomato is broken down, add rinsed quinoa and water
  4. Cover pot and after 10 minutes, take lid off and add salt
  5. Once most of the water evaporates, you can turn off the stove and serve the quinoa
