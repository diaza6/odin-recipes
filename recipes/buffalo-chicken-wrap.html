Buffalo Chicken Wraps

A great dinner to go

In a hurry? This recipe will show you how to make a filling wrap for when you're hungry on the go

Ingredients

  • 2 flour tortillas
  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1/2 tablespoon olive oil
  • A pinch of paprika, cayenne pepper, black pepper, and garlic powder
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1 slice of swiss cheese
  • 6 pickle chips
  • Buffalo sauce of your choice
  1. Heat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Add spices and olive oil to chicken breast
  3. Bake for 20 minutes
  4. Heat flour tortillas
  5. Add 3 slices of avocado, half of the swiss cheese slice, and 3 pickles to each tortilla
  6. Once chicken is done, cool for 2 minutes, and shred (easier with forks)
  7. Add shredded chicken to each tortilla, add buffalo sauce, and wrap it up
  8. Voila! You now have buffalo chicken wraps to enjoy anywhere. It can be eatten cold
